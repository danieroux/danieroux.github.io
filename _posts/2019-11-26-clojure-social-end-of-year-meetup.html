At the Clojure end-of-year social meetup, I asked the question: “What is the one piece of content you want everyone else to be aware of”. These were the answers:

  • The Good Place, https://www.nbc.com/the-good-place/exclusives/tgp-podcast
  • Explain on Netflix: https://www.netflix.com/za/title/80216752
  • World War II in Colour: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II_in_HD_Colour
  • Listen to anything by https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grigory_Sokolov
  • Dan Carlin's Hardcore History Podcast: https://www.dancarlin.com/hardcore-history-series (especially https://www.dancarlin.com/product/hardcore-history-wrath-of-the-khans-series)
  • Factfulness: Ten Reasons We're Wrong About the World - https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/34890015-factfulness
  • The Mind is Flat: https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/36325702
  • The seminal book about macros: https://letoverlambda.com
  • Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality: http://www.hpmor.com - via https://www.lesswrong.com
  • A thought experiment: https://rationalwiki.org/wiki/Roko's_basilisk
  • The Black List: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Blacklist_(TV_series)
